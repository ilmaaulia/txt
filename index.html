About recent discoghrapy:

The Chaos Chapter: FIGHT or ESCAPE

Fighting against the chaos and uncertainties of life or running away from them is the central idea of The Chaos Chapter: FIGHT or ESCAPE. The EP illustrates the difficulties and obstacles people encounter in daily life and encourags them to defend themselves or flee harmful circumstances.

The Chaos Chapter: FIGHT or ESCAPE is a strong and dynamic EP that highlights TXT's artistic diversity and capacity to communicate complicated emotions through music.

Minisode 2: Thursday's Child

The EP looks at the idea of youth and the various feelings and experiences that go along with it. The phrase "Thursday's Child Has Far to Go" is taken from an old nursery rhyme, and it serves as a metaphor for the members' artistic development and journey. Ultimately, Minisode 2: Thursday's Child is a skillfully produced and cogent EP that highlights TXT's musical diversity and artistic development.

The Name Chapter: Temptation

The Name Chapter:TEMPTATION shows a young person on the cusp of adulthood. The lads feel a yearning to put off their growth and stay in Neverland after going through strife and instability. Their progress in spite of this desire serves as a metaphor for the generation of today as they continue to develop despite the obstacles in life.

The Name Chapter: TEMPTATION consists of five tracks total: lead single “Sugar Rush Ride,” “Devil by the Window,” “Happy Fools (feat. Coi Leray),” “Tinnitus (Wanna be a rock),” and “Farewell, Neverland.”

About TXT

A young and energetic boyband with the catchy moniker TOMORROW X TOGETHER hopes to become the next big international act. Each member of the band, SOOBIN, YEONJUN, BEOMGYU, TAEHYUN, and HUENINGKAI, is unique from the others and shines in their own way to create a cohesive whole.

The Dream Chapter: Star, an extended play (EP), served as their debut on March 4, 2019. The EP was the highest-charting debut album by a male K-pop group at the time, debuting and peaking at number one on the Gaon Album Chart, Billboard Global Albums Chart, and US Billboard 200. TXT became the second-fastest K-pop group to debut on the latter and the first with its lead single "Crown," which debuted at the top of the Billboard Global Digital Songs list and at number one on the Billboard Emerging Artists chart.

A number of new artist awards were given to the group as a result of their early commercial success, including Rookie of the Year at the 34th Golden Disc Awards and the 2019 Melon Music Awards, New Artist of the Year at the 9th Gaon Chart Music Awards, and Best New Male Artist at the 2019 Mnet Asian Music Awards.
